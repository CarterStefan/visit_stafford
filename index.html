Welcome To Stafford.

Staffordshire's historic County Town with its castle and visitor centre and the Ancient High House, the largest timber framed town house in England and one of the finest Tudor buildings in the country, where King Charles I stayed. Enjoy the vibrant market square, location for many of the town centre events, the Gatehouse theatre, the fantastic restaurant and shopping offer and of course the renowned friendliness of Stafford's population. For centuries, travellers by stageoach and narrowboat have broken their journeys to enjoy all that Stafford has to offer. Today you'll find it even easier to reach by road, rail and air from every corner of the globe.
